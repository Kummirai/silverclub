The Home Of Young Adults

A club that provides a supportive environment for Seventh Day Adventist members who are 31 and above. This club aims to foster spiritual growth, social interaction, and community service.

Join Us

Who Are We?

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where young adults can explore their faith, build meaningful connections, and find inspiration to lead purposeful lives. We are committed to fostering a sense of community and fostering personal growth as we journey together in faith.

Activities

Our Activities

Hiking

Embrace the serenity of the great outdoors as we trek through lush trails, discover hidden vistas, and relish the tranquility of nature. Whether you're an experienced hiker or new to the trails, this journey promises something spectacular for everyone.

Bible Study

Join us for a Bible study journey that promises fellowship, illumination, and growth! Whether you're a seasoned scripture explorer or just starting your journey, this study welcomes everyone with open arms and open hearts.

Prayer Breakfast

As the sun paints the new year with hope, join us for a gathering where faith fuels inspiration and breakfast nourishes the soul. We invite you to a Prayer Breakfast. Its more than just a meal – it's a promise of encouragement, inspiration, and a renewed sense of purpose.

Bible Reading

As the new year beckons with possibility, what better way to embark on a journey of growth and discovery than by immersing ourselves in the timeless wisdom of the Bible.

Silver Camp

Ready to swap screens for sunshine and swap routine for epic adventure? Then buckle up, because the Silver Camp is blasting off, and you're invited! Dive deep into the camp through games, workshops, and campfire chats that'll light a fire in your soul.

Sports

Unleash your inner athlete: Whether you're a track star, a hoops fanatic, or a master of the beanbag toss, there's a challenge waiting for you. Come test your skills in a thrilling lineup of games and activities for all ages and abilities.

Dinner

More than just burgers and drinks, this night is about inspiring talks, games that spark your mind, and activities that ignite your soul.Ditch the screens and rediscover the power of face-to-face fun. Make new friends, catch up with old ones, and build memories that'll last a lifetime

Sabbath Lunch

Good food and even better company? Let's break bread and swap stories.

I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me

Philippians 4 : 13 NKJV

This verse reminds us that we have the ability to endure trials and hardships when we keep our eyes on Jesus. It’s not about what WE can do, but rather about what GOD can do. When Paul wrote “I can do all things through Christ” he didn’t mean it as a promise that things will go our way. Instead, he wrote this verse to give us hope in the midst of our struggles and trials.
